Rock Paper Scissors

Game Manual

Apart from interacting with this game using a mouse, you can also use the following keyboard shortcuts!

  • Press 'r' for Rock
  • Press 'p' for Paper
  • Press 's' for Scissors
  • Press 'a' for toggling Auto Play
  • Press 'backspace' for Reset Score
  • Press 'm' to toggle manual
